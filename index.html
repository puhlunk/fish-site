Our Story

Started in Nashville with a passion for elevating the humble French fry, Bag Lady's Fry Joint has been serving up creative loaded fries since 2020.

Using locally sourced ingredients whenever possible and preparing everything fresh daily, we pride ourselves on quality and flavor in every bite.

From food truck beginnings to our brick-and-mortar location, we're grateful to the Nashville community for their continued support.
