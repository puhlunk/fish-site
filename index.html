Our Story

Since 1972, Ed's Fish House has been a staple of North Nashville, serving up crispy, golden catfish and whiting with soul, heart and hometown flavor.

What started as a small neighborhood spot quickly became a local legend, known for our plates, bold seasoning and fresh fish.

For over 50 years, we've proudly kept the tradition alive, dishing out the kind of meals that bring people back week after week.. generation after generation!
